About BUETK

Balochistan University of Engineering & Technology Khuzdar (BUETK) is a premier institution dedicated to providing quality education in engineering and technology. Established with a vision to foster innovation and research, BUETK aims to produce highly skilled professionals who can contribute significantly to national and global development.

Our commitment extends beyond academics, focusing on holistic student development, ethical values, and community engagement. We pride ourselves on a vibrant campus life, state-of-the-art facilities, and a distinguished faculty dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders.

Admission Information

How to Apply

BUETK offers admissions in various undergraduate and postgraduate programs. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and accessible.

  • Step 1: Check Eligibility Criteria for your desired program.
  • Step 2: Fill out the Online Application Form accurately.
  • Step 3: Upload all required documents (transcripts, certificates, etc.).
  • Step 4: Pay the Application Processing Fee.
  • Step 5: Appear for the Entrance Test (if applicable).
  • Step 6: Await Admission Decision and Merit List.

Important Dates

  • Application Start: July 15, 2025
  • Application Deadline: August 31, 2025
  • Entrance Test: September 15, 2025
  • Merit List Announcement: September 30, 2025
